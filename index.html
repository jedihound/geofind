How GeoFind works

Not just a map — a geological inference engine

📍
Confirmed deposit pins
Every solid pin is a verified USGS MRDS deposit with GPS coordinates, commodity data, development status, and direct links to the full USGS record.
🔷
Geological inference zones
If agates are found where Columbia River Basalt outcrops, every other outcrop of that same formation in the region is shaded as a probable collecting zone — even undocumented spots.
🌐
Community data scraping
AI searches rockhounding forums, gem club trip reports, and hobbyist blogs to extract real-world field finds that no official database captures.
🛰
Satellite view on demand
Every deposit pin links directly to Google Maps satellite view so you can see the exact terrain, road access, and surrounding geology before you drive out.
⚡
Ask Claude about any site
Click Ask Claude on any deposit or zone for detailed collecting tips, formation geology, what tools to bring, and what specimens to look for.
🔍
Live search — coming soon
Type any location worldwide and GeoFind will dynamically query USGS, scrape community sites, and run geological inference in real time for your area.
